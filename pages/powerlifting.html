About Powerlifting Collaborations and contact

I remember getting frustrated about not making progress in the gym. I really wanted to give up, and so I gave myself an ultimatum. If I were to spend all this time in the gym and not make progress, then it was not worth it. This led me to seeking out a powerlifting coach to work with- and that is how I got started in the sport.
